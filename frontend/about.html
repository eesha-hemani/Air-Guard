About Air Guard

Our Mission

Air Guard is a smart health monitoring platform designed to help people with asthma understand and manage their environmental risk factors.

Using real-time air quality data, personal triggers, and environmental conditions, Air Guard predicts asthma risk levels and provides preventive insights for safer breathing.

Team Code Blue

Team Code Blue is a group of developers passionate about combining technology and healthcare innovation.

Our goal is to build digital solutions that improve respiratory health and help people live safer, healthier lives.
